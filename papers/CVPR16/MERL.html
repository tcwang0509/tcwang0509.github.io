Supplementary Material for SVBRDF-Invariant Shape and Reflectance Estimation from Light-Field Cameras

Results on Entire MERL Dataset

We provide shape and reflectance estimation results for all 100 materials in the MERL dataset, both on a sphere and a complicated shape. We compare the results to PLC [1], SDC [2], and PSSM [3]. It can be seen that our method achieves good results on most material types except fabric, which does not follow the half-angle assumption. However, for all the material types, we still outperform the other methods.
